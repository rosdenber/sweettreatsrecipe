Turon

Turon, also known as banana lumpia or banana spring rolls, is a popular Filipino snack or dessert. It is made by wrapping slices of ripe cooking bananas (usually saba variety), along with occasionally adding a slice of jackfruit, in a spring roll wrapper and then frying until golden and crispy. The wrapped banana is typically coated with caramelized sugar, giving the turon a sweet and slightly sticky exterior.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

12 mins

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Make sure the banana has enough sugar coating it by rolling it on the sugar plate.

step 2

Put some ripe jackfruit inside the spring roll wrapper along with the sugar-coated banana.

step 3

After folding and locking the spring roll wrapper, seal the edge with water.

step 4

Heat the oil in a pan and add the sugar. Allow the brown sugar to float.

step 5

Place the banana inside the wrapper and cook it until the wrapper gets golden brown and the excess sugar clings to it.

step 6

Serve hot as a dessert or snack. Share and Enjoy!

ingredients

6 pieces bananas saba or plantains, cut in half (lengthwise)

1 cup jackfruit ripe, sliced

1 1/2 cup sugar

12 pieces lumpia wrapper

2 cups cooking oil
