Mais con Yelo

Mais con yelo is a refreshing and popular Filipino dessert that translates to "corn with ice" in English. It is a simple and delightful treat enjoyed especially during hot weather in the Philippines. The dish features sweetcorn kernels served over crushed ice and generously drizzled with a variety of sweet toppings.

prep time

20 mins

cook time

0 mins

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Mix evaporated milk and sweetened condensed milk in a medium bowl, then transfer the mixture to a container with a spout. Set it aside.

step 2

Combine creamed corn and whole sweet kernel corn (if used) in a large bowl.

step 3

When ready to serve, use an ice shaver to create fine shaved ice: place ice cubes in the machine and shave until fine. Freeze any shaved ice until assembly.

step 4

In a halo-halo glass or a tall glass with a wide rim, transfer shaved ice, filling about 1/4 of the glass. Add a layer of approximately 2 tablespoons of corn mixture. Follow with another layer of shaved ice, then corn mixture, and finally more shaved ice.

step 5

Pour around 1/3 cup of the evaporated milk mixture over the ice and serve immediately, or serve with the evaporated milk mixture on the side. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.

ingredients

1 cup evaporated milk

1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk

1400 ml can cream style corn

1/2 cup whole sweet kernel corn drained, optional

5 cups ice cubes
